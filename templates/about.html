Born on the 04th of February, 1985, in Loule, Portugal. Since I can remember, I love computers and technology in general.

Although my passion, my career and formal training until now was in Marketing Management working with big brands as, Henkel, Danone, Coca Cola, Pepsi, KFC, Gordon’s, Johnny walker, Smirnorff, Bacardi, OREO, etc.

During my journey, I growth from Trade Marketing Assistant until Business Director, achieving a very successful path in the Marketing Agencies world.

Even though I had this amazing ride, at the end of the day I was not happy and fulfilled, I felt something was missing.

I found in coding something that I never felt before, was not a “job” was a pleasure seating in a computer and building backends.

I start learning more and more about it and never stoped, nor I want to stop!
